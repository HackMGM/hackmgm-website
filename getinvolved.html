Participate in Projects

Code for America Brigades use technology to improve their local communities. Visit our Github page to learn more about our civic technology projects and to connect with us. We invite individuals of varying skill levels and talent to help. Technologists can provide project management expertise, mentoring, code reviews, or assistance with documentation. Civic-minded individuals can host an event or engage in promotion and marketing to the community. Anyone can suggest project ideas, support our strategic plan, and offer creative presentations related to technology, government, or learning.

Corporate Contributions

If you are looking to contribute at a corporate level, please share your ideas of how you would like to contribute. Examples of contributions include giving a tour and introduction of your company, sponsoring an event night with snacks and drinks, telling us about job opportunities, and providing meeting space at your facility for collaborations and meetups.

Attend Brigade Events

Join our Meetup, and attend brigade events. HackMGM hosts a monthly lunch meetup and a regular monthly brigade meeting. The brigade also sponsors other technology and civic related events.
